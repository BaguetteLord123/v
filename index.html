Cabinet Support

With the Bambu Lab A1 mini, I created a custom support pin to hold up a cabinet shelf by utilizing basic 3D modeling (e.g. extruded boss/base and cut). I measured the tolerance using a measurement tape to find the dimensions (4.5in x 1.5in x 1in).
